Hero: Demons slayer

Hero interest

My anime hero is Tanjiro Kamado from Demon Slayer. He inspires me because of his kindness, determination, and loyalty to his family. Even after facing tragedy, he never gives up on finding a cure for his sister Nezuko and continues to fight demons with courage and compassion.

Breathing Style:
Tanjiro uses Water Breathing techniques and later develops Sun Breathing.
Goal:
He travels to find a cure for Nezuko and to defeat Muzan, the Demon King.
Strengths:
His strong sense of smell, determination, and empathy make him a powerful Demon Slayer.
