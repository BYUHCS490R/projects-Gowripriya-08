Different types of Food items

Indian food is famous for its spices, regional variety, and strong flavors. North Indian dishes often feature breads and rich curries, while South Indian cuisine centers on rice, lentils, and coconut flavors. Coastal areas serve fresh seafood and unique coastal curries
