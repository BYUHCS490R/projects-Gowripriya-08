Biryani

Biryani is the best food in the India, where ever you go you in india you can see biryani, every flavour in it reperesents the india culture. it might varies in some places but the texture and ingredients are same everywhere even the process too. if you come to india, you will for sure became a big fan to biryani.

Biryani is a delicious and popular rice meal made with scented basmati rice, tender meat or vegetables, and a blend of concentrated spices. Biryani is cooked on slow heat to blend all the spices together, thus making it tasty and special. Biryani is often served on festivals, weddings, and family gatherings. It smells amazing, looks colorful, and tastes spicy and flavorful. Biryani is enjoyed by everyone with raita or salad alongside.
